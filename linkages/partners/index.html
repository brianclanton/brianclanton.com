Linkages to Learning Partners

The bedrock of the Linkages to Learning initiative is the collaboration of public and private partners. These include:

  • Catholic University School of Nursing
  • Children's National Medical Center
  • Family Services Agency
  • GUIDE Youth Services
  • Holy Cross Hospital
  • Mental Health Association
  • Montgomery County Public Schools
  • Montgomery County Department of Health and Human Services
  • Montgomery County Collaboration Council for Children, Youth and Families
  • Rockville Youth Services
  • Silver Spring Branch YMCA Youth Services

Community Links

Collaboration among families, schools, community agencies, etc., is essential to a student's academic success when offering nonacademic services to children and their families at school sites.

In addition, each Linkages to Learning site has established partnerships with local groups such as churches, synagogues, police and recreation departments, volunteer programs, service organizations, arts and literacy councils.
